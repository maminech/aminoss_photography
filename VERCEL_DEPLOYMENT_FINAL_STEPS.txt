STEP 1: ADD DOMAIN TO VERCEL (3 minutes)
================================================================================

1. Open your browser and go to:
   https://vercel.com/dashboard

2. Find your project: "aminoss_photography" or "aminossphotography"
   Click on it

3. Click "Settings" (top menu)

4. Click "Domains" (left sidebar)

5. In the "Add Domain" box, type:
   innov8.tn

6. Click "Add"

7. Vercel will show you DNS records. Copy these (you'll need them for Step 3):
   
   Record 1:
   Type: A
   Name: @
   Value: 76.76.21.21 (or whatever Vercel shows)
   
   Record 2:
   Type: CNAME
   Name: www
   Value: cname.vercel-dns.com (or whatever Vercel shows)

8. Keep this page open!

================================================================================
STEP 2: UPDATE ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES (5 minutes)
================================================================================

Still in Vercel dashboard:

1. Click "Settings" (if not already there)

2. Click "Environment Variables" (left sidebar)

3. Find and UPDATE these 3 variables (click Edit button for each):

   Variable 1: NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL
   ┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
   │ Value: https://innov8.tn               │
   └─────────────────────────────────────────┘
   Environment: Production, Preview, Development (check all 3)
   Click "Save"

   Variable 2: NEXTAUTH_URL
   ┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
   │ Value: https://innov8.tn               │
   └─────────────────────────────────────────┘
   Environment: Production, Preview, Development (check all 3)
   Click "Save"

   Variable 3: INSTAGRAM_REDIRECT_URI
   ┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
   │ Value: https://innov8.tn/admin/dashboard/photos │
   └─────────────────────────────────────────┘
   Environment: Production, Preview, Development (check all 3)
   Click "Save"

4. After updating all 3 variables, click "Deployments" (top menu)

5. Click the "..." menu on the latest deployment

6. Click "Redeploy"

7. Click "Redeploy" again to confirm

8. Wait 2-3 minutes for deployment to complete

================================================================================
STEP 3: UPDATE DNS RECORDS (5 minutes + 1-4 hours propagation)
================================================================================

Now you need to update DNS at your domain registrar.

Option A: If you registered innov8.tn with a registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.)
------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Log into the website where you bought innov8.tn
   (Could be: GoDaddy, Namecheap, OVH, 1&1, etc.)

2. Find "DNS Management" or "Domain Settings"

3. Look for existing A and CNAME records for innov8.tn

4. DELETE or UPDATE the existing records:
   
   Update A record:
   Type: A
   Host/Name: @ (or leave empty or put innov8.tn)
   Points to/Value: 76.76.21.21
   TTL: 3600 (or Auto)
   
   Update CNAME record:
   Type: CNAME
   Host/Name: www
   Points to/Value: cname.vercel-dns.com
   TTL: 3600 (or Auto)

5. Click "Save" or "Update"

6. Wait 1-4 hours for DNS to propagate (usually 1-2 hours)

Option B: If DNS is managed by your hosting provider
------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Log into your hosting cPanel

2. Find "Zone Editor" or "DNS Zone Editor"

3. Find innov8.tn domain

4. Click "Manage" or "Edit"

5. Update the records as shown in Option A above

6. Save and wait for propagation

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Issue: "Domain already exists"
Solution: The domain is already added. Just proceed to Step 2.

Issue: "DNS verification pending"
Solution: This is normal. Complete Steps 2 & 3, then wait for propagation.

Issue: Site not loading after 4 hours
Solution:
- Check DNS records are correct
- Try clearing browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
- Try incognito/private browsing mode
- Check https://www.whatsmydns.net/#A/innov8.tn

Issue: SSL certificate error
Solution: Wait another hour. Vercel provisions SSL automatically (takes 5-60 min)

Issue: "This site can't be reached"
Solution: DNS not propagated yet. Wait longer or check DNS records.

Issue: Old WordPress site still showing
Solution: DNS still pointing to old hosting. Verify DNS records are correct.
